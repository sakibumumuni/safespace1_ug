ℹ️ How Email Alerts Work
Two separate alert systems send emails independently:

PHQ-9 Check-in flags — Generated when a student completes the standardized PHQ-9 questionnaire before speaking to a counsellor. Includes their self-reported state and severity score (/27).

AI Periodic flags — Claude AI (Haiku 4.5) analyses mood trends, journal entries, and activity patterns on intervals: Urgent (24h), Concern (48h), Watch (72h). You can also manually trigger a check above.
